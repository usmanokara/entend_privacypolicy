Account Deletion – Entend

At Entend, we respect your privacy and offer a straightforward way to delete your account and associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account using one of the following methods:

  • Open the Entend app.
  • Go to Settings > Account.
  • Tap Delete My Account and confirm.

Alternatively, email us at info@entend.io using your registered email with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

What Will Be Deleted?

  • Your profile and personal information
  • All app usage data, settings, and messages (if applicable)

Data may be retained for up to 10 days for legal or security reasons before final deletion.

If you need any help or have questions, feel free to reach out to us at info@entend.io.
